Listen to Georgian Poetry

Audio books are slowly taking place in our lives. However, few people in Georgia use it yet.

There are a number of audio recordings of the poets featured on the posters reciting their poems. These posters promote Georgian poetry and photographs.

Poverty all around the world


In everyday life, we often forget that there are many countries around the world where people are on the verge of poverty.

These posters remind us that there are still children who are hungry and thirsty. Each of them shows a specific continent, a photo showing the poverty of this continent, below is the reason and a QR code that will take you to the donation link.

Give new life to a photo

Old black-and-white photos can be found in each of our homes. We have probably all wondered how colorful it would be.

So, why not colorize and give new life to a photo when Photoshop allows us to do so?

Zero Waste wil save the world

One of the most pressing topics today is environmental pollution.

The things we use in our daily life remain in the ground for more than a century. These minimalistic posters suggest alternatives on how to avoid environmental pollution.

Minimalism - Line art

The more time passes, the more popular minimalism becomes. These posters are designed with line art trends and respond to minimalism.
